Interests & Sundries

This is a kind of friend request. I've moved to a new town and am making a point to get off of the computer more. Also, I've managed to not have a nearby girl friend for too many years—so let's be friends!

Holler at me if you like some of these things, too.

JS & Open Source

Building tools to lower barriers for others, let's hack together. GitHub!

Architecture, Industrial Design & Urban Design

My degree is in architecture and I love the science of cities. Jan Gehl and Enrique Peñalosa are my heroes.

Cooking

Heidi Swanson's 101 Cookbooks is my go-to and Sarah Britton (My New Roots) and Anna Jones, too.

Sewing & Making

I love it. I love finding places like Purl Bee that offer well documented guides and used to maintain a sewing blog, ECAB.

Fashion, Celebrities & Nails

Red carpet gowns! Any dress from Elie Saab. I zen out watching Lisa Eldridge #nailconf!

Interiors and Shops

I love Remodelista and their shop guides are my first stop before visiting a new city.

Also—riding bikes, walking, coffee, flea markets, thinking about jogging but not jogging, BBC history podcasts, BBC history documentaries.

Jessica Lord is an open web developer, designer and urbanist in Portland.
